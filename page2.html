Grammar Focus: Present Simple for Jobs

Questions:

  • What do you/they do? / What does he/she do? (Job)
  • Where do you/they work? / Where does he/she work? (Place)
  • What do you/they do exactly? / What does he/she do exactly? (Activity)
  • How do you/they like it? / How does he/she like it? (Opinion)

Answers:

  • I am a/an... / He/She is a/an... / We/They are...
  • I/We/They work in a/an... / He/She works in a/an...
  • I/We/They help... / He/She helps... (Verb + s/es/ies for he/she/it)
  • I like it because it's... / He/She likes it because it's...
  • My job is... / His/Her job is... / Their jobs are...
