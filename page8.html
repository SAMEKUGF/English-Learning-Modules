Sports Vocabulary & Verbs

Which Verb to Use: PLAY, GO, or DO?

In English, we use different verbs with different sports and activities:

  • Use PLAY for team sports, ball sports, and competitive games where you play against another person/team (e.g., soccer, tennis, chess).
  • Use GO for activities ending in "-ing" (e.g., swimming, hiking, skiing).
  • Use DO for individual activities, non-team sports, martial arts, and general exercise (e.g., karate, judo, aerobics, archery).
